ABOUT ME

Nice to meet you.

The first time I was introduced to the Dada art movement I was filled with excitement and purpose; ever since then I have been driven by the banshee cry of art. Originally coming from a background in Art History, I became drawn to the field of UX Design by its merging of the conceptual with the beautiful. I believe that the digital sphere has the capability of changing lives and the world for the better. It is my mission to contribute meaningful content to this effort.

I am inspired by Constructivism and Minimalism and fueled by well-made cappuccinos. I love Italian wine and can cook a mean fish.
