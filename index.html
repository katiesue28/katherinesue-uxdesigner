MY ETHOS

I am a Chicago-based User Experience Designer and Researcher working to create exemplary digital experiences for clients and users through insightful research, captivating design, and a dash of quirk. I believe that open communication between designer, team, and client is vital to creating a subtly magnificent experience for the user.

PROCESS

Great work cannot be accomplished without great ideas. I take great care in sketching, wireframing, and prototyping until I reach the best solution. Design is not static; it is constantly evolving to become something even better.

COLLABORATION

Everyone has their own, unique talents and ideas; only with collaboration can these ideas be fully explored and built upon into something amazing. I have ample experience working in teams and with clients, always in a personable manner.

EMPATHY

Understanding the user's wants, needs, and pain points is vital to creating a sucessful user experience. Through my user research and design I work to compassionately create solutions that make the user's experience simpler and more beautiful.
